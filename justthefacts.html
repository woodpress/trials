Just the Facts
1
Have a good photo handy
You’ll need to upload a photo that makes you easy to recognize (like a school picture or passport photo).
2
What the new SAT costs
$43 for the SAT, and $54.50 for the SAT with Essay. If you have a fee waiver, you’ll need to enter its 12-digit code and your counselor’s name when you register.
3
Where to test
The SAT is given at local high schools and test centers. During registration, you’ll be able to select the most convenient location.
